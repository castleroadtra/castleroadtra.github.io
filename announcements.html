Announcements

Upcoming Social Pottery Event in Kentish Town

Join us for a fun two-hour pottery painting session in Kentish Town, just a short walk from the estate! This event is open to everyone, and the TRA will provide transport for those who need it and subsidize the cost.

Provisional Dates:

  • 15th September
  • 29th September
  • 13th October
  • 20th October

Final date will be chosen based on interest. Please let us know if you are interested and your preferred date.

Community Barbecue - 31st August

We're excited to announce a community barbecue on the 31st of August, following the success of our last barbecue in May 2023. We can accommodate all dietary requirements, so please let us know if you are attending and if you have any specific needs.

Feel free to bring a dish to share with everyone!

AGM

Our Annual General Meeting will be held in January (more details closer to the date). We will be discussing the budget for the upcoming year and electing new committee members. Please join us to have your say.
